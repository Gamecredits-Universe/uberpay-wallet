UberPay Wallet
===============

Our goal is to develop and consumer friendly multicoin wallet that will support the UberPay Bitcoin Kiosks. A wallet that is used on a day to day purchases


Coins Pending for inclusion:

- MazaCoin
- StartCoin
- MaxCoin
